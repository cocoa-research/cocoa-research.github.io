CoFI2024 Workshop https://cofi2024.github.io

Held in conjunction with the ICMI2024 ACM Conference, the workshop aims to explore how interactive technology can enrich dining experiences and to build an interdisciplinary community around the topics of related to commensality and human-food interaction. A special focus is on the role of multimodal interaction among commensal partners sharing food, being humans or artificial dining companions (such as social robots).
